Who Are We?

APAL SP AUTO Sdn Bhd (1549262W) is a Sungai Petani based company, operating a one stop automotive service centre in a spacious and well equipped workshop. We specialized in a wide range of automotive services, from maintenance to sales. Our team have the skills and many years of experience to work on most domestic and imported vehicles. We are committed to provide top notch quality work with the very best price and an exceptional customer service that are based on honesty, integrity and professional expertise with the aim to obtain a satisfactory outcome for our customers.

Our History

APAL SP AUTO Sdn Bhd success story begin in 20018. DR Rusli Othman (founder and owner) started off the business with Kedai Tayar APAL SP, a small enterprise focusing on car tyre services in a modest workshop. Within a year of operation, Kedai Tayar APAL SP steadily becomes the preferred workshop not only among locals but also customers from neighbouring regions. Our revenue increase positively within the first two years which then allow us to expand our operation. Our first big leap in expanding the business was in 2020, where we made significant investment in purchasing new tools, machinery and hiring specialist mechanics to improve our technical capabilities. Hence from then onwards, we are able to offer a more comprehensive service by providing auto maintenance, repairs and installation works to the consumers. In parallel, our procurement strategy also changes, we started to procure parts directly from the manufacturers (rather than third party agents) to ensure a more sustainable and cost effective parts inventory

During the challenging period of the covid pandemic in 2021, we made a bold move by acquiring the facility which we previously rented. By owning the building we are able to further expand our operation. This is a true testament of our strength and resilient as a company. When most operators are struggling to sustain, APAL SP AUTO were able to grow from a modest tyre workshop to a one stop automotive service centre.

WHERE WE ARE NOW

Realising that a small enterprise cannot effectively manage such a sizeable operation, decision was made to convert the enterprise into a corporation. In January 2024 the incorporation process was completed thus giving birth to APAL SP AUTO Sdn Bhd. APAL SP AUTO Sdn Bhd incorporated with a pay up capital of RM1Million, will now operate in a more structured manner guided by a comprehensive work management system. Ensuring the company sustainability and continued growth.

WHAT'S NEXT?

The sky is the limit, we want to strive to be among the best in the national automotive industry. Our near term target is to venture into the imported used cars sales. We are putting a lot of effort into making this a reality. A comprehensive work plan is in place for every single step of the process. Starting with the application of Open AP through MITI which we target to be submitted by Q3 of 2024 and subsequently setting up the necassary infrastructure in order to run the business.
